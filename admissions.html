Admission Requirements

üìã Required Documents

  • Completed application form with recent photograph
  • Original Birth Certificate (B-Form/CNIC)
  • Copy of student's B-Form (both sides)
  • Parent/Guardian CNIC copies (both sides)
  • Previous academic records & report cards
  • Original leaving certificate from previous school
  • Character certificate from previous institution
  • Two recent passport-size photographs
  • Medical fitness certificate (if requested)

üéØ Eligibility Criteria

  • Age appropriate for the class applied
  • Satisfactory academic record from previous school
  • Pass entrance test with minimum 60% marks
  • Good conduct certificate from previous school
  • Parent commitment to school policies
  • Completion of all admission formalities
  • Payment of admission fee upon acceptance
  • Attendance at orientation program

üìö Class-Specific Requirements

  • Primary (Class 1-5): Basic interview & assessment
  • Middle (Class 6-8): Written test in core subjects
  • Matric (Class 9-10): Science/Arts stream selection test
  • FSC (Class 11-12): Pre-Medical/Pre-Engineering entrance test
  • All tests cover English, Urdu, Mathematics
  • Science test for relevant classes
  • Islamiyat & Pakistan Studies assessment
