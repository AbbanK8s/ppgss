About PPGSS

Pakistan Public Girls Secondary School (PPGSS), established in 1995, is a distinguished educational institution dedicated to providing quality education to young girls from Primary level through FSC (Faculty of Science). Located in Pakistan, we pride ourselves on our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic growth.

Our institution combines traditional Islamic values with modern educational methodologies, preparing our students to become confident, knowledgeable, and responsible citizens. With state-of-the-art facilities and a highly qualified faculty, we ensure every student receives personalized attention and support.

PPGSS maintains a nurturing environment where girls can flourish academically, socially, and spiritually. We believe in empowering young women through education and building a strong foundation for their future success.

Admissions 2026

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate (B-Form)
  • Previous academic records
  • Recent passport-size photographs
  • Parent/Guardian CNIC copies
  • Entrance test (for relevant classes)
  • Original leaving certificate from previous school

Admission Process

  • Submit application form with documents
  • Appear for entrance test/interview
  • Await admission confirmation
  • Pay admission fee upon acceptance
  • Collect fee vouchers and schedule
  • Attend orientation session
  • Start classes as per academic calendar

Important Dates

  • Application Start: January 15, 2026
  • Application Deadline: February 28, 2026
  • Entrance Tests: March 1-15, 2026
  • Result Announcement: March 25, 2026
  • Fee Submission: March 26 - April 10
  • Academic Session Starts: April 15, 2026
